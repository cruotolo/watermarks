As described in the methods section, these various papers provided ideal conditions for watermark capture, as they were unbound and in most cases devoid of printing. I searched on these pattern types in the Gravell watermark archive (see the archives section) and while I didn't find any dead-on matches, I did find several analogues for my images, some quite striking -- see, for example, one of the hand watermarks from the Gravell collection juxtaposed against the hand watermark on one of the Book Arts Press leaves.
